Voter Instructions
  1. Ensure you are a registered voter before logging in.
  2. Use your registered Email ID and Password to login.
  3. OTP verification is mandatory for secure authentication.
  4. Each voter can cast only one vote.
  5. Your vote is confidential and securely encrypted.
  6. Once submitted, the vote cannot be changed.
